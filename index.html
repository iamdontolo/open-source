About Us

The Smile Network

The Smile Network is a Nonprofit Oraganization that caters for children under the age of 10 that are lacking the previledge of Child Empowerment, Education, Livelihood and Healthcare for the purpose of a sustainable community for the future generation and the growth of a better tomorrow.

Our Team of volunteers and partners from around the world are doing extremely the best in combating these challenges for the children on the streets that are not having parental traces. We want to encourage every helping individual or organization to come onboard and join the course.
